Sharing Circle Guidelines

Our sharing circles are designed to provide a safe, respectful space where men can share their personal experiences and reflect on their physical, emotional, intellectual, and spiritual well-being. We conclude by expressing gratitude.

Use "I" Statements

By using "I" statements, you ensure that the reflection is personal, and that you take ownership of your experiences. This approach deepens the level of sharing in the circle and creates a safe space for authentic, personal expression.

PEISG Framework

1. Physical (P)

Focus: Your body, health, and energy levels.

Questions to reflect on:

  • "How is my body feeling this week?"
  • "What physical activities have I been doing, and how have they affected me?"

2. Emotional (E)

Focus: Your feelings and emotional well-being.

Questions to reflect on:

  • "How have I been feeling emotionally?"
  • "What’s one emotion I have experienced this week, and how have I handled it?"

3. Intellectual (I)

Focus: Your mental activity, learning, and growth.

Questions to reflect on:

  • "What have I learned recently, and how has it impacted me?"
  • "Have I faced any mental challenges this week? How have I dealt with them?"

4. Spiritual (S)

Focus: Your sense of purpose, inner peace, and connection.

Questions to reflect on:

  • "Do I feel connected to my purpose right now?"
  • "What helps me feel grounded or at peace, and how can I do more of that?"

5. Gratitude (G)

Focus: Reflecting on positivity and what you appreciate.

Questions to reflect on:

  • "What’s one thing I am grateful for today?"
  • "Who or what brought me joy recently, and how did it affect me?"
